About
  • This string generator combines two strings to produce interesting portmanteaus.
  • A portmanteau word is a linguistic blend of words, in which parts of multiple words or their phones (sounds) are combined into a new word.
  • Example: smog, coined by blending smoke and fog; motel, from motor and hotel; Liger, from Lion and Tiger
  • This generator parts the given strings around the vowels present. These are syllables of the word.
  • Output string is produced by combining prefix of string 1 and suffix of string 2.
  • LENGTH: drag the ranger to adjust length.
  • RESET: resets all the strings.
  • SUBMIT: press everytime after changing the length.
